Create sticker
To create a sticker, click the Right Mouse button. Hold the Right Mouse button to select the color of the sticker.
Delete sticker
To remove the sticker, Right Mouse click on it. To delete all stickers, press Ctrl + Del
Move sticker
Drag the sticker with the Left Mouse button.
Save stickers
Do not need anything. Stickers are saved in automatic mode.
